Transferring to Tufts

We welcome transfer students from community colleges and four-year institutions. Our transfer admission process is designed to recognize the work you've already done and help you build on it here.

Before you apply, use our credit transfer tool to get a clear picture of where you stand. Upload your transcript and we'll show you which courses are likely to transfer — so you can plan your degree from day one.

What transfers?

Most lower-division coursework from regionally accredited institutions is eligible for transfer credit. Common transfers include:

  • General education requirements (writing, math, sciences)
  • Introductory major coursework
  • Elective credits (subject to department approval)

Credits are evaluated course-by-course. Our tool gives you a preliminary assessment — the official evaluation happens after you apply.
