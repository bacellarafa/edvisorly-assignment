At Tufts, individuals from every state and around the world come together to form a community of curious, engaged peers who challenge and encourage each other. Our admissions team is committed to finding and bringing to Tufts students with the potential and motivation to change the world.

You can find your place at Tufts, whether you're fresh out of high school, ready to engage in graduate-level academic work, or preparing to take the next leap in your career. Open your mind, further your exploration, and set yourself on the road to success.

Transferring to Tufts

We welcome transfer students from community colleges and four-year institutions. Our transfer admission process is designed to recognize the work you've already done and help you build on it here.

  • General education requirements (writing, math, sciences)
  • Introductory major coursework
  • Elective credits (subject to department approval)
